Overview of FRC Programming

You're here because you want to program a robot, but in reality it's far more complex than that. Robots arent just one thing, they're made of many moving parts working together to perform a function. these parts are called subsystems and they're the organs that make up the organism that is the robot, but even these have programmable parts of their own. Motors, sensors, rotors, servos, even LEDS, the basic electrical and locomotive components that must each also be made to work in the way you want. Your goal is to take all of these components, make them work, and stitch them all together.
