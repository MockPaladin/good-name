Structure of a Robot

Robots consist of many different parts called subsystems which each perform their own role for the robot. These are things like the drive, pick-up, and vision functions. After subsystems come the smaller components that they're made of such as motors, servos and sensors which act as the basic electrical components for the robot's functions.
