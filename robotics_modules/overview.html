You may want to get started right away, but a robot is much more complex than just one program think but in reality it's far more complex than that. Robots aren't just one thing, they're made of many moving parts working together to perform a function. these parts are called subsystems, but even these have programmable parts of their own. Motors, sensors, rotors, servos, even LEDS. you need to try and make these work, and stitch them together. In the end, you need to compile and stitch all these programs together.
